The Scientific Inacuracies in Project Hail Mary by Andy Weir

I would like to start this off by saying Project Hail Mary, by Andy Weir is my absolute all time favourite book. The only other possible contender to this position would be Weir's 2015 bestseller, The Martian.

Like any hard science fiction, I often pondered about the scientific whilst reading this book. Using the internet to prove or disprove my hunches, whilst simultaneously gather with others who also enjoyed it as much as I did to share and discuss out opinions on the events.
